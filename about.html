Any Questions?

Why did you make this website?
I was working on a Neural Network Model and I wanted to deploy it on the web, it was then that I found TensorFlow.js.

I plan to publish my blogs, and display my projects as I work on them on this website.

How long have you been practicing machine learning?

I discovered the wonder that machine learning is while watching the Keynote (Google I/O '18) video on YouTube. Back then I never understood anything remotely related to Machine Learning. That hooked me to machine learning, and I’ve been learning and practicing it ever since!

I have also touched upon Deep Learning, and played around with Neural Networks for Image Recognition, Classification and Natural Language Processing.

Why are you interested in NLP / machine learning?

I am curious by nature. Machine learning and natural language processing are both excellent tools to understand the world better.

I strongly believe that there is untapped potential to machine learning that I would like to tap into, to make the world a better place.

Why are you interested in data visualization?

Along with being curious, I am also creative and analytical by nature. I love visualizing data and finding patterns in them too.

I trust that data must be visualized effectively and efficiently in order to extra the knowledge that it possess.

What kind of work do you see yourself doing?

My passion lies in building machine learning applications that can help make existing processes easier and more efficient. I also love communicating with people, drafting out business plans and visualizing data.

I describe my perfect future position to be a mix of business analytics data science, and machine learning engineering. Essentially a business analyst with the skills to deploy machine learning models.
